How to Use: Stepper

<nav aria-label="Stepper">
  <ol>
    <li><span>1</span><div>Step Name</div></li>
    ...
  </ol>
</nav>

<!-- Linear Stepper with Progress Bar -->
<div>
  <span>1</span>
  <div>progress bar</div>
  <span>2</span>
</div>

<!-- Vertical Stepper -->
<ol style="flex-direction:column;">
  <li><span>1</span> Step One</li>
  ...
</ol>
  • Use <ol> for ordered steps and aria-label for accessibility.
  • Theme steps with .axiom-btn and CSS variables.
  • Combine with progress bars for linear workflows.
  • Vertical steppers for forms or onboarding flows.
