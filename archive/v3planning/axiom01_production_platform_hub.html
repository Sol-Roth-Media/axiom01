The Semantic Rebellion

Why are we loading hundreds of kilobytes of third-party JS runtimes and maintaining thousands of brittle CSS utility variables simply to align elements? Axiom01 trusts the platform.

01 / SPECIFICITY GONE

Native Cascade Isolation

We bundle framework structures under native @layer constraints. Your application-level custom overrides always succeed cleanly without needing raw specificity combat or the !important emergency break.

02 / FLUID GEOMETRY

Breakpoint-Free Ratios

Margins, container query card pivots, and editorial columns automatically scale using mathematical proportions driven by clamp() formulas and Container Queries.

03 / SEMANTIC FORMS

Zero-JS Input Proxies

By nesting inputs inside semantic <label> blocks, focus rings, invalid error inputs, and contextual offsets are computed natively in the browser layout pipeline using :has().
